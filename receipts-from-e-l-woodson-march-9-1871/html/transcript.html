Jackson Miss
March 9th 1871

Due Vicksburg & Meridian R R transportation from Jackson to Meridian for Forty one Soldiers & two citizens on account of State of Mississippi

Emmet L. Woodson

Jackson Miss
March 13th 1871

Due Vicksburg & Meridian R R transportation from Jackson to Meridian for three (3) citizens on account of State

Emmet L. Woodson
